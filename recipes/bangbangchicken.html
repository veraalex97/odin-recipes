Bang Bang Chicken Recipe

Bang Bang Chicken by Diana71

This bang bang chicken is a wonderful Americanized version of an extremely popular Sichuan Chinese dish.

With twice-fried chicken, you get the crispy fried breading you'd expect, and the bang bang sauce drizzle makes the entire dish spectacular.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • ¼ cup potato starch
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon shichimi togarashi (Japanese red pepper condiment)
  • 2 cups oil for frying, or as needed
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
